Report local issues. Get them seen.

Broken streetlight? Garbage not collected? Pothole on your street? Report it here so it's visible to everyone in the community, not just lost in a phone call nobody follows up on.

Why this exists

Most local issues — a broken streetlight, uncollected garbage, a dangerous pothole — get reported once, verbally, to someone who may or may not follow up. There's no shared record. This tool creates one: every report is logged with who reported it and what the issue is, and stays visible on the public log until it's marked resolved.
